Sambutan Bulan Keluarga Kebangsaan 2024

The National Month celebration took place on November 16, 2024, at the Pavilion Hall, LPPKN Family Center, Seremban 2, Negeri Sembilan. The event, themed around classic attire, brought together 38 SEMANIS members from UTM alongside representatives from other public universities. Participants enjoyed a variety of traditional games, accompanied by a lucky draw. This program served as a platform to foster closer bonds between SEMANIS members and the broader Negeri Sembilan community.
